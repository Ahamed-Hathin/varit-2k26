About

National Level Technical Symposium

VARIT stands for a combination of various IT-related events. The name was coined in 2011 by students of our IT department. The first VARIT event was conducted in 2011 and has since been held annually, organized by final-year students with support from first-year and second-year students. How many of us know? Our college does not charge any registration fee from participants, and we provide food free of cost. VARIT is a proud legacy of Jamal Mohamed College, organized by the CS, IT, and CA departments.

    REGISTRATION RULES &
    REGULATIONS

    ALL THE PARTICIPANTS ARE COMPULSORILY ADVISED TO READ OUT THE INDIVIDUAL EVENT REGULATIONS WITHOUT FAIL

    General Instruction:

    • Maximum of 12 UG (BCA, B. Sc C.S / IT / AI & ML, B. Voc (I.T), B.E (CSE / AI & ML / AI & DS), B. Tech (IT)) students can participate from a department.
    • PG Students are strictly not allowed.
    • Only two Departments per college will be permitted. First come First basis
    • A participant cannot take part in more than two events.
    • The participants should must bring their ID card and bring a Bonafide certificate from their college.
    • No registration Fee.
    • Spot registration is allowed.
    • Lunch and snacks will be provided
    • Prelims will be conducted for all the events, if needed, depending on the entries.
    • The decision of the Judges will be final and binding.
    • The participants should come in neat and formal dress.
